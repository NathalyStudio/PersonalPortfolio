Who am I?

I'm a Frontend developer with two years of experience and through my skills in web development and design I help everyone who needs to find, change or adapt their voice to the digital world.


No matter if you are a small or big business, an agency or a SEO; if you need a right hand in design, layout and development of your projects, I can help you.


And how can you be so sure that I can achieve any goal that you propose to me? Because I am constantly updating myself on trends and best practices in user experience and user interface design, frontend development (frameworks, CSS, animations).

How is my work process?

1. Identify

I help to discover what is the main problem or what do you want me to solve.

2. Brainstorm

With the situation identified, comes the process of brainstorming to expose all possible solutions adjusted to the requirements.

3. Analize and pick

All options are studied and the one that best suits the needs of the current project is chosen.

4. Sketch and prototype

I create the wireframes and high fidelity designs and prototypes the interfaces of each screen.

5. Implement

I start the implement and deployment process.

6. Test

I make sure that everything is covered and working properly with different tests and deliver the final product.
